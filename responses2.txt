#### 1. What is the difference between a Trojan, worm, virus, and botnet?

A trojan is an application that appears legitimate but contains hidden functionality that can be used by attackers to compromise systems or steal data. A worm is a self-replicating program that spreads from computer to computer without user intervention. Viruses are programs that attach themselves to other files on your system and execute when you open them. Botnets are networks of computers infected with malicious software (malware) that allows remote users to control those machines as if they were their own.
